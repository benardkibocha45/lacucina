    Tranquil Dining by the Forest

    Nestled in the leafy Thigiri neighborhood near Karura Forest, Cotton Tree Eatery & Bar offers a serene escape with a culinary twist.

    • Specializing in Asian fusion cuisine, especially authentic Vietnamese dishes.
    • Enjoy signature meals like grilled pork with rice noodles, shaken beef, and vegetarian spring rolls.
    • Elegant indoor and breezy outdoor seating with rustic-modern décor — perfect for brunch, lunch, or dinner.

    Located at 114 Thigiri Lane, Nairobi, we’re open daily from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Whether you're craving bold flavors or a peaceful dining spot, Cotton Tree Eatery & Bar welcomes you with warmth and creativity.

    Cajun Chicken Wrap

    Tender chicken strips marinated in Cajun spices, wrapped in a soft tortilla with fresh veggies and a tangy aioli.

    A perfect mix of spice and crunch, served with seasoned fries and house salad. A lunchtime favorite!

    Grilled Halloumi Salad

    Fresh greens tossed with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and topped with perfectly grilled halloumi slices.

    Drizzled with a lemon-herb vinaigrette, this salad is a refreshing, light yet filling option for vegetarians and cheese lovers alike.

    Cotton Tree Burger

    Juicy beef patty stacked with cheddar cheese, crispy bacon, caramelized onions, and house sauce in a brioche bun.

    Served with crispy fries and a pickle spear. It’s a towering burger that lives up to its name!

    Prawn Coconut Curry

    Succulent prawns simmered in a creamy coconut curry infused with lemongrass, ginger, and fresh herbs.

    Served with jasmine rice and a side of sautéed vegetables. A bold, aromatic dish with coastal flair.

    Sticky Toffee Pudding

    A warm, moist sponge cake made with dates and drenched in toffee sauce.

    Served with a scoop of vanilla ice cream, this decadent dessert is a comforting finish to your meal.
